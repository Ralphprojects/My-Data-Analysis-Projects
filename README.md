### Results/Findings
- Data Engineers earn the highest with a salary of 138,285,531 USD in 3 years.
- Sales Data Engineer and BI Data Engineer earns the lowest  with salary of 60,000 USD in 3 years.
-The medium size company earned the highest with salary of 632,295,031 USD in 3 years.
- Small size companies earn the lowest with salary of 14,354,173 USD in 3 years
- Companies in the United States earns the highest among the countries with salary of 606,726,833 USD in 3 years.
- Companies in Indonesia earns the lowest with the salary of 15,000 USD in 3 years.
- Full-Time employees earned the highest salary in the span of 3 years.
- Year 2023 produced highest salary scale with a total of 546,537,896 USD.
- Year 2020 produced the lowest salary scale of 7,517,399 USD.

### Recommendations
 Role-Based Recommendations
Insight: The stark gap suggests either underutilization or undervaluation of Sales/BI Data Engineers. Consider aligning their roles more closely with revenue-driving functions.
Company Size Recommendations
Insight: Medium-sized companies may have the agility of startups and the resources of large firms—ideal for innovation and growth.
Geographic Recommendations
Insight: U.S. dominance suggests strong market maturity. Indonesia’s low figures may reflect emerging market dynamics—opportunity for strategic development.
Employment Type Recommendation
- Full-Time roles yield the highest returns.
Recommendation: Focus on full-time hiring for core roles; use contract/freelance for peripheral tasks.
Time Trend Recommendations
Insight: The surge in 2023 suggests recovery or expansion. Use this data to forecast and plan future hiring and investment cycles.

### Strategic Summary
- Invest in high-performing roles (Data Engineers).
- Support underperforming roles with training or restructuring.
- Scale medium-sized companies for optimal ROI.
- Expand in high-yield regions like the U.S.; develop emerging markets like Indonesia.
- Prioritize full-time employment for stability and performance.
- Use time-based insights to guide budgeting and hiring strategies.
